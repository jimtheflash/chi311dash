Background

Chicago 311 Service Requests

  • Chicago, has a program for soliciting service requests from residents, called 311

  • Residents can dial 311, or use the 311 website or app, to request service from a menu of options

  • All 311 service requests, and associated metadata like location, are available via the Chicago Data Portal, a repository of public data sets

Chicago Community Areas

  • The city of Chicago is be divided into 77 distinct neighborhoods called community areas

  • Residents often identify strongly with their community areas

  • While community areas don’t map directly onto electoral districts, they are often useful
